The indicator Proportion of important sites for terrestrial and freshwater biodiversity t hat are covered by 
protected areas, by ecosystem type shows temporal trends in the mean percentage of each important 
site for terrestrial and freshwater  biodiversity (i.e., those that contribute significantly to the global 
persistence of biodiversity) that  is covered by designated protected areas  and Other Effective Area -based 
Conservation Measures (OECMs) . 
 
  
Protected areas, as defined by the IUCN (IUCN; Dudley 2008), are clearly defined geographical spaces, 
recognized, dedicated and managed, thro ugh legal or other effective means, to achieve the long -term 
conservation of nature with associated ecosystem services and cultural values.
