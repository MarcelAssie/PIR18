The renewable energy share in total final consumption is the percentage of final consumption of energy 
that is derived from renewable resources.  
 
  Last updated:  2024-03-28 
Renewable energy consumption includes consumption of energy derived from: hydro, wind, solar,  solid 
biofuels, liquid biofuels, biogas, geothermal, marine and renewable waste. Total final energy 
consumption is calculated from balances as total final consumption minus non -energy use.  
 
Comments regarding  specific renewable energy sources:  
• Solar energy includes solar PV and solar thermal.  
• Liquid biofuel s include biogasoline, biodiesels and other liquid biofuels.  
• Solid biofuel s include fuelwood, animal waste,  vegetable waste, black liquor, bagas se and 
charcoal.  
• Renewable w aste energy covers energy from renewable municipal waste.
