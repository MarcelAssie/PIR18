An individual owns a mobile cellular phone if he/she has a mobile cellular phone device with at least one 
active SIM card for personal use. Mobil e cellular phones supplied by employers that can be used for 
personal reasons (to make personal calls, access the Internet, etc.) are included. Individuals who have only 
active SIM card(s) and not a mobile phone device are excluded. Individuals who have a mobile phone for 
personal use that is not registered under his/her name are also included. An active SIM card is a SIM card 
that has been used in the last three months.
